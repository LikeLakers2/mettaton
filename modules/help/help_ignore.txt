```rp!ignore```
**Usage**:
rp!ignore `<add/remove/list>` `[@user1]` `[@user2 @user3 ...]`

**Parameters**:
`1.` The action to take against the ignore list.
`2.` The users to add or remove. Must be mentions

**Description**:
Manages the bot's ignore list. Any users on this list will not have their usages of commands parsed.
