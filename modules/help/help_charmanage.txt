```rp!charmanage```
**Usage**:
rp!charmanage `<action>` `[parameters]`

**Parameters**:
The action you wish to take, as well as any parameters if needed. The available actions are as follows: `view`, `set`, `setprop`, `list`, `search`, `delete`

**Description**:
Allows you to manage your characters, as well as giving RP admins access to manage the character list in the RP. For more info on an action, use `rp!help charmanage <action>`.

**Aliases**:
rp!cm
