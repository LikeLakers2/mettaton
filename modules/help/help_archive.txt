```rp!archive```
**Usage**:
rp!archive `[number of messages]`

**Parameters**:
Number of messages to archive. Optional, defaults to 250.

**Description**:
Archives the last specified number of messages (defaults to 250 if not specified) in that channel to a file, and sends it. Useful if you've just had something exciting happen and want to archive it for later.

Admins can specify "all" as the number of messages, to archive an entire channel.
