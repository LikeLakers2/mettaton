```rp!archivechan```
**Usage**:
rp!archivechan `<add|remove|list>`

**Parameters**:
Action to take upon the auto-logging list.

**Description**:
Manages the auto-logging feature of this bot. To add a channel to the auto-logging system, use `rp!archivechan add` in the channel you wish to add. To remove a channel, use `rp!archivechan remove` in that channel. To list off the channel IDs that this bot is auto-logging (including those from other servers), use `rp!archivechan list`.
