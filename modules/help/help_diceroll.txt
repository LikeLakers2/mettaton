```rp!diceroll```
**Usage**:
rp!diceroll `[[dice]d[sides]]`

**Parameters**:
The diceDsides to roll, in a DND format. Optional, defaults to 1d20, or a single 20-sided die.
Example: `rp!diceroll 3d20` to roll 3 d20's, or three 20-sided die.
You are limited to 100 dice per roll, and 1000 sides per dice.

**Description**:
Roll a dice. It's simple. The bot will output how many of each number it rolled if there are more than 10 dice rolled.

**Aliases**:
rp!roll, rp!dr
